The HalloweenCon™

Welcome to The HalloweenCon™

It is the official organization that organize contest every year on the eve of Halloween.
Every year we organize contest and choose the best 3 among the participants.
The main contest is the Best Halloween Costume of the Year.
Many participants contest every year by dressing up for the Halloween.
The 3 most scary and horrifying costume is chosen and the participants are given the price accordingly.
Besides contest, the organization deals in Decorations, Collectibles, Accessories, Costumes (Girls, Boys, Toddler, Women, Men), Animatronics, etc. for the Halloween Event.
So what are you waiting for?
Register Yourself for the contest.
Dress Up Scary and Horrifying and who knows you will be the Contest Winner of Halloween 2022.

Contest Rules

The participants must adhere to the following rules:-
  1. The participant have to dress scary and horrifying on the eve of Halloween event.
  2. Just register yourself on the site.
  3. Fill in all the required details.
  4. Upload your selfie. The selfie must be of high-resolution like 1080p or higher is better.
  5. No fees is required for the contest.
  6. The organization will choose the best 3 participants who dressed scary and horrifying on Halloween.
  7. The winners will be announced on October 3, 2022.
  8. The first winner will be awarded a cash price of $1000.
  9. The second winner will be awarded a cash price of $500.
  10. The third winner will be awarded a cash price of $100.
  11. The registration starts from 25 October and ends on 30 October.
