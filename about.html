About The HalloweenCon™

The HalloweenCon™ is a fictional organization.

This was created as part of a Halloween Contest from W3Schools.com on the Halloween Eve in October 2022.

The website was created by Mr. Jimmy Jayant.

Its main purpose is to organize contest on the eve of Halloween.

Also the organization have a store that deals in Halloween merchandise.

The organization also have the online presence on social networking platform like facebook, twitter, etc. and also on Instagram.
